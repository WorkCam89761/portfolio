The Sleeper Truenas Server

Internal Components
External View

A sleeper PC build that is aesthetically pleasing on the outside that has an I7 1280p, 32gb of ram, 5 6tb 7200 rpm drives in zfs RaidZ2 with two 1tb ssds caching to make the read speed faster. An external 2.5 GB ethernet usbc port for faster network speeds. An EVGA 850 GQ 80+ Gold PSU, and finally, a Sparkle intel arc a380. This runs on truenas scale os.
